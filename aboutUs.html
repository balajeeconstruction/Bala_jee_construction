About Us

jai Bala Jee Construction started it’s business in the field of construction in 2016 individual flats , bungalows and G+3 floor houses in Delhi and Jharkhand . In 2017 it established itself as Ms jai Bala Jee Construction and taken up large commercial and housing projects and Govt Projects. The company is fast evolving as EPC contractor having expertise in all fields of construction be it construction of Piling foundation, RCC structure, Hard and soft finishing, all MEP services, Sanitary & plumbing work and Aluminum & Glazing work along with different type of facade screens .Another dimension to the company is it’s interior designing wing which does take turn key contracts for designing and execution. The Company also undertakes structural, Architectural design along with design of services for housing project. Company has a tie up with many talented consultants. We in short provide a single window solution for building industry. The Company is backed by large pool of talented administrative, technical and commercial staff. The company uses all modern and highly advance construction techniques with the help of state of art construction equipments and has a rich pool of highly skilled, semiskilled and unskilled construction workers. Directors of the company are highly qualified and have vast experience in the field of Administration, Commercial, financial and technical. Jai Bala jee Construction. is committed towards excellence, quality, system, environmental health and safety. The USP of the company is its strong vendor base and exceptional resourcefulness for workforce of all trades and unmatched speed.
